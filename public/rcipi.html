About Paneer roll:

In this paneer roll recipe, the stuffing is a lip-smacking paneer tikka made on skillet,
a vibrant green chutney and a crunchy mixed veggie salad. The outer wrap is made
with whole wheat dough. Overall, this is a nutritious and delicious vegetarian paneer
kathi roll recipe!

  • Paneer
  • Roll
  • Vegiies
